Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Math Mastery cost?

Math Mastery's core content is completely free! We believe in providing high-quality math education accessible to everyone. We do offer a premium subscription with additional features like downloadable resources, advanced problem sets, and personalized tutoring for $9.99/month.

Is Math Mastery aligned with school curriculum?

Yes! Our content is aligned with common core standards and most international curricula. We cover topics from basic arithmetic through calculus,

Do I need to follow a specific schedule?

Not at all! Math Mastery is designed for self-paced learning. You can study whenever it's convenient for you, focus on specific topics you need help with, or follow our recommended learning pathways. Our system remembers your progress so you can pick up right where you left off.

Is Math Mastery suitable for all ages?

Yes! We have content suitable for elementary school students just beginning their math journey all the way through college-level advanced topics. Our adaptive system ensures learners of all ages and skill levels receive appropriate content and challenges.

How does the personalized learning work?

Our adaptive learning technology analyzes your performance on practice problems and quizzes to identify strengths and areas for improvement. Based on this data, we customize your learning path, providing more practice in challenging areas and advancing you through topics you've mastered. This personalized approach ensures efficient and effective learning tailored to your specific needs.

Can teachers use Math Mastery in their classrooms?

Absolutely! We offer special accounts for educators with additional features like classroom management tools, assignment creation, student progress monitoring, and detailed analytics. Many teachers use Math Mastery to supplement their curriculum, provide differentiated instruction, or assign homework. Contact us for information about school and district licenses.
