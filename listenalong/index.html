FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Schedule 1 Calculator

1

What is the purpose of the Schedule 1 Calculator?

The Schedule 1 Calculator helps players simulate and test different combinations of base products and substances to determine the resulting effects, costs, and sell prices before applying them in the game.

2

Can the Schedule 1 Calculator handle special recipes?

Yes, the calculator can recognize special recipes and apply predefined costs and sell prices for those combinations, ensuring accurate results for unique mixes.

3

How does the Schedule 1 Calculator calculate the sell price?

The calculator uses the effects of the mix to determine a total multiplier, which is then applied to the base product's price to calculate the final sell price. Special recipes may have fixed sell prices.

4

Is the Schedule 1 (Mixing) Calculator customizable for new substances or effects?

Yes, the calculator is designed to be easily updated with new substances, effects, and interaction rules by modifying its JSON data files.

5

How does the mixing system work in Schedule I?

The mixing system involves combining drugs with additives (like food, medicine, or chemicals) to alter their effects and value. The quality of the ingredients affects the strength and price of the final product. Experimenting with different combinations is crucial for maximizing profits and creating unique products.

6

Can I play Schedule I with my friends?

Yes, Schedule I supports up to 4-player online co-op. You can manage your empire together, divide tasks such as manufacturing and distribution, and coordinate your strategies. Make sure all players are using the same version of the game (either the free trial or the paid version)
